Fudgy Brownies

Description

These homemade fudgy brownies are rich, dense, and incredibly chocolatey. With a perfect shiny, crackly top and a moist, fudgy center, these brownies are made with cocoa powder and chocolate chips for an intense chocolate flavor that's better than any box mix[3][5].

Ingredients

  • 10 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1¼ cups granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ⅔ cup chocolate chips (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F and line an 8-inch square pan with parchment paper[3].
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium-low heat[3].
  3. Add sugar, cocoa powder, vanilla, and salt to the hot butter. Stir well[3].
  4. Let mixture cool slightly, then add eggs one at a time, stirring vigorously[3].
  5. Add flour and stir until well combined[3].
  6. Fold in chocolate chips if using[3].
  7. Spread batter in prepared pan[3].
  8. Bake for 20-30 minutes until edges look dry and middle is slightly underbaked[3].
  9. Cool completely before cutting[3].
