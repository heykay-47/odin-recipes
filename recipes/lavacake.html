Chocolate Lava Cake

Description

This decadent chocolate lava cake features a rich chocolate exterior with a warm, gooey molten center that flows out when cut. Ready in less than 30 minutes, it's the perfect impressive dessert for special occasions or whenever you're craving an intense chocolate treat[4].

Ingredients

  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 6 ounces bittersweet chocolate, chopped
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F. Butter and flour 4-5 ounce ramekins[4].
  2. Melt butter and chocolate together until smooth[4].
  3. Beat eggs, egg yolks, sugar, and salt until thick and pale yellow[4].
  4. Fold chocolate mixture and flour into egg mixture[4].
  5. Divide batter among ramekins[4].
  6. Bake for 6-8 minutes until sides are firm but center is jiggly[4].
  7. Let cool for 1 minute[4].
  8. Invert onto serving plates and remove ramekins[4].
  9. Serve immediately with powdered sugar and berries if desired[4].
